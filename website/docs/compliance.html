Compliance Frameworks

RedForge maps every probe to regulatory and standards frameworks — automatically generating compliance gap reports from scan results.

⚠️
RedForge provides technical compliance assessment, not legal advice. Consult qualified legal counsel for official compliance determinations.

Mapping Overview

Enable compliance reporting with --compliance NIST_AI_RMF,EU_AI_ACT or in your YAML config:

bash
redforge scan --provider openai --authorization research \
  --compliance NIST_AI_RMF,EU_AI_ACT,ISO_42001

NIST AI RMF

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework maps across four core functions:

FunctionCategoryRedForge Coverage
GOVERNAI Risk GovernanceAuthorization controls, audit logging
MAPAI Risk IdentificationAll 47 probes map to risk categories
MEASUREAI Risk AnalysisCVSS-style risk scoring, ASR metrics
MANAGEAI Risk ResponseRemediation guidance in HTML reports

Key NIST AI RMF controls assessed:

  • MS-2.5: Red team testing — covered by all 47 probes
  • MS-2.6: Adversarial testing — covered by GCG, PAIR, multi-turn attacks
  • GV-1.7: Risk monitoring — compliance gap reports

ISO/IEC 42001

AI Management System standard. RedForge probes map to Annex B controls:

  • B.5.2: AI system testing — probe-based vulnerability assessment
  • B.6.1: Data quality — training data memorization probes
  • B.7.4: AI system robustness — robustness and adversarial probes
  • B.8.4: Intended use boundaries — topic bypass, excessive agency
