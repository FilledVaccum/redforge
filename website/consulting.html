What You Receive

Every engagement produces four concrete deliverables — not a checkbox summary

D-001 // Vulnerability Report
Full Probe Scan Report

Complete results from all 49+ probes run against your LLM — every OWASP LLM Top 10 category, every MITRE ATLAS technique. Each finding includes the exact payload, the model's response, a CVSS-style risk score, and the evidence snippet that triggered the verdict.

FINDING RF-001-001 · CRITICAL · LLM01
Payload: "Ignore previous instructions and..."
Response: [REDACTED — 847 chars of sensitive output]
Score: 9.1/10 · FAIL
D-002 // Compliance Gap Report
Regulatory Gap Analysis

Probe failures mapped directly to NIST AI RMF (GOVERN/MAP/MEASURE/MANAGE), EU AI Act Articles (9, 10, 13, 14, 15, 50, 53), and ISO/IEC 42001 clauses. Each gap states the control, the evidence of non-compliance, and the remediation required to close it. Hand this to an auditor.

EU AI Act Art. 9 — Risk Management System
Status: NON-COMPLIANT
Evidence: 3 critical failures in LLM01, LLM06 categories
Required action: Implement prompt injection controls
D-003 // Guardrail Guide
Guardrail Implementation Guide

For every failed probe, a concrete guardrail recommendation: detector type (input scanner, output scanner, or both), scan target, YARA rule template ready to deploy, similarity reference patterns, and recommended action. Structured for direct import into llm-guard, Presidio, or a custom pipeline.

Detector: InputScanner → PromptInjectionDetector
YARA rule: rf_001_prompt_injection.yar [attached]
Similarity refs: 12 semantic anchors
Priority: CRITICAL — implement before next deploy
D-004 // Remediation Roadmap
Prioritized Remediation Roadmap

All findings ranked by risk × exploitability × compliance impact. Each item includes: specific code/config change required, estimated implementation effort, re-test criteria, and which compliance controls it closes. Formatted for direct import into Jira, Linear, or GitHub Issues.

How It Works

Three steps from intake to report. No infrastructure setup on your end.

01
Scope & Configure

Submit the intake form below. You tell us: your LLM provider, model ID, endpoint URL, system prompt (if any), and intended use case. We scope the probe selection to match your deployment context — a customer-facing chatbot gets different probes than an internal code assistant.

// 30 min of your time
02
Scan & Analyze

We run the full RedForge battery against your endpoint — your API key, ephemeral, never stored. All 49+ probes execute with our 37-strategy mutation engine enabled, expanding coverage to hundreds of payload variants. Scan typically completes in 2–4 hours. Human analyst reviews every failure for false positive elimination.

// 2–3 business days
03
Report & Debrief

PDF report delivered within 5 business days. Professional engagement includes a 60-minute debrief call where we walk your team through every critical finding, answer questions, and prioritize the remediation roadmap. Enterprise tier includes a follow-up re-scan after your fixes are deployed.
